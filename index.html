Purpose

Our purpose is to render a professional service in according with your requirement to protect your property on a 12 or 24 – hour’s basis. Our trained personal strive to limit the risk of any threat to the clients assets. Officers will have the necessary training and skill to do their job the best they can. Training is essential in our changing industry and A.R.Z Security officers are required to continue to improve their skills with fitness training and necessary skills to provide the best service. Through training, performance evaluations, and quality assurance measures, we ensure that security officers maintain standards consistent with A.R.Z Security Services We are the first line of defends against criminal elements.



Physical Fitness Standards

Security offers physical fitness improves offers and organizational safety. Physical fit security officers are less likely to have their authority questioned and less likely to be physical challenged. On the other hand, when security personnel need to exert themselves, like having to run a distance, or to run up a flight of stairs, or to physical defend themselves, or others against an assault, physical fit security offers are able to maintain an adequate level of physical stamina necessary to overcome an attackers and survive the encounter conversely, unfit security officers are often danger to themselves and others and become an “added” liability when theirs required to physically defend themselves.

Recruitment

Security today demands personnel and management who are competent and alert to the changing environment. Our recruitment policy stipulates certain education standard. All application are carefully screened and criminally checked with the Private Security Industry Regulatory Authority and are required to have knowledge of the Industry standard. Supervisors and Management Formally test the knowledge of the Security personnel and additional training is then introduced to rectify any of these identified weaknesses.



Security Standards

Our standard are to provide a service where the officers will be Equipped for their task. They will be positively motivated both physically and mentally. Our officers will report for duty on time, sober, alert, neat in appearance and capable of responding to instructions as set out in the Job Description.



Operational

Prior to commencing our services we will liaise with the client to identify individual and specific needs and these will be incorporated into comprehensive work procedure. These work procedures are laid out in constitutes the job Description. The Job Description remains on site with the officers. By following these procedures, any new officer joining the site knows exactly what the specific duties are for the site.



Inspections

We visit our sites and security personal on a regular, unscheduled basis to ensure productivity of our personal and value for money for the client. These inspections are noted in an occurrence book and controlled by management.
